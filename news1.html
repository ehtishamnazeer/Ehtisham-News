Free Fire Hungama Karachi 2025 | Garena Offline Community Event at Lucky One Mall

Karachi, Pakistan — December 20 & 21, 2025

Garena Free Fire successfully hosted the Free Fire Hungama offline community event at Lucky One Mall Karachi. The two‑day event brought together gamers, fans and content creators, showing the growth of mobile gaming in Pakistan.

Event Activities

  • Kla’s Punch Zone
  • Alvaro’s Grenade Toss

The event featured exclusive giveaways including the Free Fire x Digimon Merch Box and appearances by popular Free Fire creators.

Free Fire Hungama Karachi 2025 proved to be a major success and strengthened Free Fire’s community presence in Pakistan.
