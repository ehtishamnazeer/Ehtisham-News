Free Fire Community Event Held at Lucky One Mall

By Ehtisham News • July 2025 • Karachi

Garena Free Fire successfully organized an exciting community event at Lucky One Mall, Karachi, bringing together gamers, content creators, and esports fans under one roof.

The event featured live gameplay, fan interactions, giveaways, and special appearances by popular Free Fire creators. Hundreds of fans gathered to celebrate the growing esports culture in Pakistan.

Such offline events play a major role in strengthening the Free Fire community and encouraging new players to step into competitive gaming.
